Membership
Annual Membership of our Mandal begins from the Ganapati Program and is valid till the Annual Picnic (from September 2017 to August 2018). PMM offers only annual membership; a 6-month membership concept is not offered. Pro-rated membership is offered only after Jan of the membership year.
Benefits of Annual Membership
Free entry to at least 6 mandal events every year (Ganapati, Dandiya, Diwali, Sankrant,, Gudhi Padwa , Annual Picnic. Game Nite and Talent Show are held every other year). Marathi/Indian food is included for these events). The programs are held at the Bharatiya Temple or at a convenient location in the vicinity.
Membership includes: Discounted entry fee for any additional mandal events. Discounted fee for advertising (Mandal website / e-mail list) your business. Preference for performing at the popular Talent Show
