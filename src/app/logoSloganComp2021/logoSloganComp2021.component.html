Logo Contest 2021
नमस्कार मंडळी,

As we all know that “Philadelphia Marathi Mandal” is celebrating the Golden Jubilee year in 2021.

The PMM 50 years committee is happy to announce the “Logo & Slogan Competition” for all the marathi community. We are inviting you all to come and participate in this competition To participate in the PMM - Logo & Slogan competition, kindly submit your entries by June 15th 2021.

There will be a winner for best Logo and Slogan.


Guidelines:

Logo

One entry / Per Person (the latest entry will be counted)
Any Text added to the Logo should only be in Marathi
Logo should have “Philadelphia Marathi Mandal” name which is mandatory plus should have something to signify 50 years/Golden Jubilee celebration Logo can be in any shape
Size - should fit in 2 inches * 2 Inches size
Format for logo - jpeg or png and High resolution.

Submit Your Logo!

Slogan

One entry / Per Person (the latest entry will be counted)
Any Text on the Slogan can be in Marathi or English
Slogan can have max 12 words
Any font can be used for the Slogan

Submit your Slogan
